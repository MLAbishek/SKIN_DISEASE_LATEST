Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AI-powered skin analysis service

What is SkinCare AI?

+

SkinCare AI is an advanced artificial intelligence platform that analyzes skin images to detect potential skin cancer and other skin conditions. Our technology uses deep learning algorithms trained on extensive medical datasets to provide accurate, instant analysis.

How does the analysis work?

+

Simply upload a photo of the skin area you want to analyze, and our AI will process the image using advanced computer vision algorithms. The system examines various characteristics including asymmetry, border irregularity, color variation, and size to provide a comprehensive analysis.

What types of skin conditions can you detect?

+

Our AI is primarily trained to detect potential skin cancer indicators, including melanoma, basal cell carcinoma, and squamous cell carcinoma. The system can identify suspicious moles, lesions, and other skin abnormalities that may require medical attention.

Is the service free to use?

+

We offer a free tier that allows 5 analyses per month. For unlimited access and additional features, we offer Pro and Enterprise plans. Check our Services page for detailed pricing information.

What image formats are supported?

+

We support JPG, JPEG, and PNG image formats. Images should be clear, well-lit, and show the skin area clearly. Maximum file size is 10MB.

How long does the analysis take?

+

Analysis typically takes 10-30 seconds depending on image size and server load. Pro users get priority processing for faster results.

Can I use the camera on my device?

+

Yes! Our platform supports both file upload and direct camera capture. Simply click "Start Camera" and take a photo directly through your device's camera for instant analysis.

What devices are supported?

+

Our web platform works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We also support mobile devices through responsive design. A mobile app is coming soon.

How accurate is the AI analysis?

+

Our AI achieves 99% accuracy in skin cancer detection, comparable to professional dermatologists. However, this tool is for screening purposes only and should not replace professional medical evaluation.

Can I use this instead of seeing a dermatologist?

+

No, this tool is designed for screening and early detection only. It should not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified dermatologist for proper evaluation.

What should I do if the AI detects a potential issue?

+

If our AI flags a potential issue, we recommend scheduling an appointment with a dermatologist for professional evaluation. Early detection is crucial for successful treatment outcomes.

How often should I check my skin?

+

We recommend monthly self-examinations and annual professional skin checks. If you have a family history of skin cancer or many moles, more frequent monitoring may be advised by your dermatologist.

Is my data secure?

+

Yes, we use enterprise-grade encryption and security measures to protect your data. Images are processed securely and are not permanently stored on our servers.

Do you store my images?

+

No, we do not permanently store your images. Images are processed for analysis and then automatically deleted. We only retain anonymous, aggregated data for improving our AI algorithms.

Is my health information private?

+

Absolutely. We follow HIPAA guidelines and never share your personal health information with third parties. Your privacy is our top priority.

Can I delete my account and data?

+

Yes, you can request account deletion at any time. We will permanently remove all your personal data from our systems within 30 days of your request.
